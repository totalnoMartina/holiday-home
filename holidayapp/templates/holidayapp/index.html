Kacjak Beach

Only 1 km north-west of the property, there is Kacjak,a small penninsula with a harbour, FKK beach, Pizzeria, Tennis Court, Mini Golf and a playground for yougsters. About 50 Camp-houses can find a spot and enjoy the summer in this Touristic Camp and/or in the Bungalows within Tourist Village 'Kacjak' where full room-service is provided. There is a Reception, Parking, a small Coffee Bar, a Fast-Food Deli right on the beach and an Ice-Cream stand to help cool of the hot summer days. There is also a restaurant with a terrace and live music during summer nights.

Restaurant Domino

In the heart of Dramalj, about 15-minute walk from the property, there is Domino, a Mediterranean seafood restaurant. There is indoor seatings and a lovely terrace surrounded with a few trees and bushes for a real natural experience. About 5 minute-walk from "Lanterna Beach" in Dramalj.

Crikvenica City

Only 5 km east of the property, there is Crikvenica, a small coastal city with vibrant nightlife and variety of bars and restaurants. The main beach is sandy and there are rocky beaches alongside the coastal area. Crikvenica was developed on the site of a Roman era settlement and military base called 'Ad Turres'. In the 19th century Crikvenica began to attract many tourists, which brought about a turning point in its history.
